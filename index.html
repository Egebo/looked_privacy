Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026  ·  Developer: Egemen Bozca (Egebo)

Overview

Looked is a mobile app that helps you manage your personal style. This policy explains what data is collected when you use the app, how it is used, and how it is protected.

By using the app, you agree to this policy.

Data We Collect

Looked collects the following information:

  • Account information: Email address, display name, and username via Google or Apple Sign-In
  • Wardrobe images: Photos of clothing items you add (stored in Firebase Storage)
  • Style data: Style quiz results and outfit history (stored in Firestore)
  • Community content: Posts and comments you share

AI Analysis

When you use the outfit analysis feature, your photo is sent to Google Gemini AI. This only happens with your explicit consent.

Google Gemini AI does not retain your photo after the analysis is complete. The image is transmitted solely for the duration of processing.

Third-Party Services

The app relies on the following external services:

  • Firebase (Google LLC)
  • Google Gemini AI
  • RevenueCat
  • Google Sign-In
  • Apple Sign-In

Each service is governed by its own privacy policy. Your data is never shared with any third party for marketing purposes.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the core features of the app
  • To manage your wardrobe and style history
  • To power community features
  • To maintain app security and performance

Your data is never used for advertising, marketing, or profiling.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account directly in the app by opening the Profile page and scrolling to the bottom.

When your account is deleted, your wardrobe images, style data, community posts, and all personal information are permanently removed.
